According to Sportsmole, Manchester United will consider signing Dutch midfielder Donny Van de Beek over Aston Villa's Jack Grealish this summer.

Man Utd thinks Villa's value for Grealish is too high considering their budget for this transfer window. Grealish has been valued above 60  million euros by Villa.

Photo Credit: Reuters

Grealish's dream move may not happen
From his recent and old tweets, it is evident that  Man United is his dream club. His move to United may, however, be halted due to the fee Aston Villa is asking for his signing. The club is also ready to extend his contract.

Ajax midfielder, Donny Van de Beek, played an important role at the club in the 2018-2019 season leading Ajax to the UEFA Champions League semi-finals. The 23-year-old has however agreed with Ajax allowing him to leave in this transfer window.

The Dutch midfielder also had a good run last season, creating 11 assists and scoring 10 goals. If Man Utd sign him, he will prove to be the perfect back up for Pogba and Bruno Fernandez.

Manchester United still have hopes of signing Jadon Sancho even though Dortmund confirmed that Sancho will stay until next season.

Ole Gunnar hopes to sign at least three high-profile players this season even though the club has a fixed budget.


 

According to Man City manager, Pep Guardiola, Eric Garcia has refused to extend his contract with the club as he wants to play in other places.

His decision comes amid reports that Barcelona want the 19-year-old centre back to move to Camp Nou this summer.

"He announced to us that he doesn't want to extend his contract with Manchester City," said Guardiola in his latest press conference.

"He has one more year and after that, he doesn't want to extend. We want it but he doesn't want to extend it, so I imagine he wants to play in other places."


With only Clement Lenglet and Gerard Pique expected to be at Barcelona next season, the club is currently looking for a centre back to boost its defence.

Barcelona are reportedly strapped for cash and are therefore expected to offer Man City €10 million plus add ons.

Garcia had a fantastic start after the lockdown overtaking the likes of Otamendi and Fernandinho in the starting line-up. The youngster is also likely to start against Real Madrid on Friday in the Champions League.

Man Utd coach, Ole Gunnar, had a fantastic run in the recent completed EPL season finishing third to secure Champions League spot for United next season.

Solskjaer believes ending the season with a trophy will be a good start for him with all focus put on the Europa League that scheduled to resume tomorrow.

"We're four games away from winning a trophy," Ole Gunnar said at a news conference.

Man Utd will face LASK Linz tomorrow in a second leg match in the round-of-16 tie. United will go into the game with a 5-0 lead.

This is what Ole Gunnar had to say in the news conference about the expected match:
"We know it will be a difficult game like it was last time — it wasn't a 5-0 difference.

"We know we have to perform and there is a chance for players to stake a claim for a place in the quarter-final if we get through."

Luke Shaw and Axel Tuanzebe will miss the match but Lingard, who scored his first Premier League goal in their final game against Leicester, will be available tomorrow.

Manchester United are looking for a world-class centre forward that will give them options in the attacking positions. Jimenez would be a perfect option for them looking at his recent performances in the Premier League.

Wolves signed Jimenez for £33m meaning Man Utd is therefore likely to pay more for him to move to Old Trafford. Across all competitions last season, Jimenez scored 27 goals. He is likely to perform even better at Man Utd looking at the kind of players United have. 

Nuno Espirito thinks Sporting de Braga striker Paulinho is a perfect replacement for Jimenez and Wolves is, therefore, closing a deal to sign him on a £27m deal.

From reports, agent Jorge Mendes has already concluded the deal. Paulinho, now 27-years-old, has scored 25 goals for Sporting de Braga in the last 48 appearances. This record was enough to convince Espirito that Paulinho is the perfect replacement.

These new developments mean that Man Utd are free to start bidding to sign the Mexican, with RTP, a Portuguese outlet, claiming that the deal is close and that Jimenez will play in the Champions League next season under Ole Gunnar.

Barcelona did not perform well to their expectations this season, with Messi openly showing how disappointed he is at the club.

They came second after Real Madrid who won the Laliga title with a game at hand. Barcelona have now placed all their hopes on the Champions League and hope to win the title.

Messi has not had a good relationship with Quique Setien for the better part of this season and could use his influence in Barca to ensure Setien is replaced before the start of 2020/2021 season.

According to the Sun, Messi believes Leeds coach, Marcelo Bielsa, is the right man for the job. With Bielsa's current contract with Leeds set to expire at the end of this season, Leeds will have no option but to let him go if he fails to sign another contract.

With Messi's influence at Camp Nou, the Leeds coach may find himself at Barca, which will be an achievement for him.

Quique Setien got a second chance earlier this month to prove himself in the Champions League after he failed to win the Laliga title.

Former Barcelona player Xavi, who is currently managing Al Sadd, is likely to get full support from the Catalan giants to manage his former club.



Former Arsenal coach, Unai Emery, has been appointed as Villareal's new head coach for the next three seasons.

He has signed a three-year deal with the club and hopes to take it to the next level. Villareal failed to qualify for the Champions League in the 2019/2020 season after ending the league in 5th place, securing Europa League spot.

Javier Calleja, now former Villareal's manager, has had a good run with the club managing to secure the Europa League spot but not to the expectations of the club's management. They have therefore seen the need for change at the club.

Unai Emery replaced Arsen Wenger at Arsenal in 2018 but failed to secure Champions League qualification, losing 4-1 to Chelsea in the Europa League finals. He was later sacked in November last year after a winless run in seven games with Arsenal. 

The Spaniard was in charge of Arsenal for just 18 months. Before joining Arsenal, he was in charge of Sevilla, Paris Saint Germain, Spartak Moscow and Valencia.

Villareal expects Emery to perform much better since he had experience in Laliga while managing their rivals Valencia between 2008-2012. He guided Valencia to three consecutive Europa League qualifications.



At 18 years of age, Mason Greenwood has achieved more compared to any other Man Utd player. His goal against Westham last night was his 18th of the season, making him the first Man Utd teenager to have hit that mark.

His style of playing is just amazing, always trying to put himself in a shooting position whenever he is close to an opponent's goal post. It is also evident that he has no weak foot as he is capable of shooting with power and precision with either foot.

Rising from the Man Utd youth academy, the teenager has been able to secure a position in the first team making regular appearances for the club. He indeed has a bright future in his football career looking at what he has been able to achieve so far.

Greenwood is rated highly in English Premier League and believes the sky is the limit for him. 

The youngster scored an important goal against Westham, a game which ended in a draw ensuring Man Utd move to position three hoping finish in the top 4 to qualify for the Champions League.

Harry Maguire termed the youngster as 'special talent' in an interview after the match. "He's been excellent; he's playing with confidence and he always seems to get on the scoresheet, especially since we've returned from lockdown.
"He's a special talent and he's got a big future at this club."

Maguire will face his former club, Leicester, on Sunday, a match that will determine their Champions League fate. A win or draw for Man Utd will be enough to qualify of the League.
